A talk by Patricia Pulles

2006.11.08
20:00〜

A talk by Patricia Pulles who is a "city curator" at the Museum Boijmans Van Beuningen in the Dutch city of Rotterdam. "City curator" is a Rotterdam concept. The curator works foremost with Rotterdam artists and designers.
Patricia Pulles will talk about the art scene in Rotterdam and especially about the exhibition "Project Rotterdam" and "Cut for Purpose" a project with urban researchers in the museum space.
