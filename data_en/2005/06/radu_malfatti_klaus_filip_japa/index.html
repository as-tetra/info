Radu Malfatti, Klaus Filip, Taku Sugimoto, Taku Unami

2005.06.04(sat)

Radu Malfatti, Klaus Filip, Taku Unami and Taku Sugimoto performed at art space tetra on saturday the 4th of June 2005. The 4 musicians performed together for 1 hour. Within that time they interplayed with the various sounds of tetra and also the sounds of the surrounding area of Suzaki Machi.
We all enjoyed their company and we hope that they will all return to tetra again.
