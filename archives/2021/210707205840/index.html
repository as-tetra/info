■ Image caption
"Tracking" 2021 / Video installation

■ Exhibition contents
Using a motion tracking camera, the video shot in real time is projected with a delay of a few seconds. A video installation that allows you to objectively view yourself by tracking the appearance of the audience and the series of shadows that are shifted in time from all angles.

■ Biography
Born in Fukuoka Prefecture in 1981
2004 Graduated from Hiroshima City University, Faculty of Arts, Department of Spatial Art
2004-05 Bildende Kunst, University of Applied Sciences and Arts in Hannover Exchange Study Abroad (Germany)
2006 Completed the master's program at the Graduate School of Arts, Hiroshima City University

■展示内容
モーション・トラッキングカメラを用い、リアルタイムで撮影された映像を数秒遅れでプロジェクション。あらゆる角度から時間軸をずらした観客の姿と影の連なりをトラッキングすることで、自己を客観視することができるヴィデオ・インスタレーション。
