Notice of Closure for art space tetra

It is with deep regret that we announce the closure of Art Space Tetra, effective April 8, 2026, due to the deterioration of the building.
We are immensely grateful for the support we have received from so many visitors over the past 22 years. Thank you from the bottom of our hearts.

We will continue to hold various exhibitions and talk events until our closing date. We look forward to seeing you there.

January 2026, art space tetra
