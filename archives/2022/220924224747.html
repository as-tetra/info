Kawada Zenkyu Furusato Kyogen Tour 2022, Fukuoka, Japan

「河田全休 ふるさと狂言ツアー2022 福岡公演」

Date: Saturday, September 24, 2022
Time: 14:30 open 15:00 start, 18:30 open 19:00 start
Place: art space tetra (2-15 Suzaki-cho, Hakata-ku, Fukuoka City, Fukuoka)

○日程:2022年9月24日(土)
○時間:14:30開場15:00開演、18:30開場19:00開演
○場所:art space tetra(福岡市博多区須﨑町2-15)

Ticket: 3,000 yen in advance, 3,500 yen at the door
*Please make reservations via the reservation form → https://forms.gle/K1hLBgB5AH7Ynzr56
Inquiries: 090-3896-5823 (Performance Executive Committee)

---------------------
Kyoto-based Kyogen performer "Kawada Zenkyu" to perform in Fukuoka for the first time!
Not only in the field of classical Kyogen, but also in the field of "Salaryman Kyogen," "Modern Kyogen Collection I-Ⅹ," "Marriage Kyogen," "SDGs Kyogen," and other works, he has been working hard to expand Kyogen's laughter to the modern age.

In this Fukuoka performance, they will present for the first time a new piece of Kyogen related to the Hakata Gion Yamakasa festival.
Whether you have never seen Kyogen or have seen it before, we hope to see you there!
----------------------

---------------------
京都在住の狂言師「河田全休」が福岡初公演！
古典芸能の狂言の分野にとどまらず、「サラリーマン狂言」『現代狂言集Ⅰ～Ⅹ』『婚活狂言』『SDGs狂言』等の作品を発表し、狂言の笑いを現代に広げる活動に力を入れています。

今回の福岡公演では、博多祇園山笠にちなんだ新作狂言を初披露します。
狂言を見たことがない方も、見たことがある方も、是非お越しください！
