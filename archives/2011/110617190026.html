Adrian Fiskum Myhr (doublebass) and Tore T. Sandbakken (drums) has played together for many years in different constellations of jazz and free-improvised music. Lately the duo has been working with departing from the traditional role of the rhythm-section. Through preparations and alternative techniques on their instruments they have created a playful and challenging sound.
