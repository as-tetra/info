- Profile
Venta Protesix
Venta Protesix is the solo project of Italo Belladonna active since 2008. Always considered as one of the most controversial personalities of the entire panorama of experimental music in Italy, for over ten years he has been moving within the most extreme computer music with performances all over Europe and Asia where he conveys a constant sensation of psychoacoustic discomfort to his listeners through live sets of digital harsh noise with strong impact. @italobelladonna

m2m
Formed in 2025, m2m is a duo consisting of programmer and media artist mgs and pianist and trackmaker Layla.
Instagram @laylamorpho
https://mgs.nyc

hospi
Electronic musician.
Lives and works in Nakatsu, Oita.
After working as a techno DJ (Baka Dojo), he started live performances mainly using synthesizers.
He has a studio in a former rice warehouse for recording and other activities.
His own fragrance brand “WAKIGA” is offered free of charge at each venue.

- プロフィール
Venta Protesix
Venta Protesix は、2008年から活動している Italo Belladonna のソロプロジェクトである。
イタリアの実験音楽界において最も物議を醸す人物の一人と常に見なされており、10年以上にわたり、極限のコンピュータ音楽の領域で活動している。ヨーロッパやアジア各地でパフォーマンスを行い、デジタル・ハーシュノイズによる強烈なインパクトを持つライブセットを通じて、聴衆に一貫したサイコアコースティックな不快感を与え続けている。@italobelladonna
