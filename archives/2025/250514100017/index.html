Company Profile

As a security company based in Kyushu, Nishikei Security Service Co., Ltd. protects the safety and security of the local community while also contributing to cultural promotion as a sponsor of art space tetra. Below is an overview of the company.

Company name 株式会社にしけいセキュリティサービス（Nishikei Security Service Co., Ltd.）
Company logo
Establishment February 4, 1994
Address 5-10 Tenya-cho, Hakata-ku, Fukuoka City, Fukuoka 812-0025, Japan (2F)
Representative Koichi Yamaguchi, President and Representative Director
Business Facility security, crowd (event) security, traffic guidance security, security and private security, sales and installation of security cameras
Major corporate shareholder Nishikei Co., Ltd.

For more information on Nikkei Security Services Co., Ltd., please visit the official website.
