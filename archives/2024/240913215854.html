ART FAIR EARTH 2024® is a project that connects countries around the world through friendship. This year, we will welcome Mr. Ahmed Tubasi, artistic director of Freedom Theatre in Palestine, as our guest and hold a workshop to create a play with local high school students. During the festival, there will be many exchange events connecting countries online, so please feel free to come and join us.
