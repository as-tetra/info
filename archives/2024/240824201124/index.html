A city that looks like something I've seen before.

何かで見たような街

The ordinary scenery that we see on a daily basis.
By removing the human touch and sense of life that should exist there and painting it on canvas
By removing the human touch and the sense of life that should exist there and painting it on canvas, a slight sense of discomfort and displacement is created even in a landscape that should be familiar to us.
I am conscious of this unrealistic sensation that can be felt in contemporary landscapes.

Rento Koganemaru
Born in Fukuoka, Japan in 2001
This is my first solo exhibition, and I will mainly exhibit large works. Please come and see my works.

普段から見ている当たり前の景色。
そこに存在しているはずの人間味や生活感を取り除いてキャンバスに描く事で
普段見慣れているはずの景色でも、少しの違和感やズレが生まれてくる。
そういった非現実的な感覚を現代の景色で感じ取れるように意識している。

小金丸廉斗
2001年福岡県生まれ
初の個展で、大きい作品をメインに展示します。よろしくお願いします。
