It is now September again and we will be holding our annual project ART FAIR EARTH.
ART FAIR EARTH® is a project that connects artists and art professionals from around the world through friendship.
This year's event will be held from September 13 to 23 with Ahmed Tubasi, artistic director of the Freedom Theater in Jenin, northern Palestine, as a guest artist. As a related project, a workshop to create a play with local high school students will be held online, and a performance will be held on September 14 to present the results.
There will also be many exchange events connecting countries online during the festival, so please feel free to come and join us.

Simultaneous Exhibitions in Vietnam and Fukuoka
100 Asian Portraits Photo Exhibition
The exhibition features interviews and photographs of Asians living in Japan who were unable to return to their home countries due to the Corona disaster. An exhibition of 100 portraits of 100 people from 18 countries including Vietnam, Nepal, Bangladesh, China, Thailand, Cambodia, Indonesia, Myanmar, Laos, Jordan, and Kazakhstan was held simultaneously in Japan and Vietnam.

Related Project②.
To the Mysterious World of Myanmar Music National Tour 2024 / Fukuoka
Myanmar music is the most distant from Western music. Myanmar music is a little-known genre in Japan, but its uniquely evolved style is both strange and beautiful. Until now, when Myanmar music has been introduced in Japan, it has mostly been in the context of world music, but that is too much of a shame. Its uniqueness can be found in free jazz/progressive/contemporary music/dub.
Murakami Kyoki, who has been conducting field research since 2016, will introduce its history and appeal by playing records, cassettes, CDs, and videos. In particular, there will be a first report on the results of his field research (noise scene and record digging) conducted in December 2023.
Please enjoy the impact of Myanmar, an unknown super music country.
　
　Venue: art space tetra
　Friday, September 13, 2024
　Time: 19:00 start (doors open 30 minutes before)

関連企画②
ミャンマー音楽の謎めいた世界へ 全国ツアー2024 / 福岡
西洋音楽から最も離れた位置にあるミャンマー音楽。日本ではあまり知られていないジャンルですが、独特に進化したそのスタイルは奇妙かつ美しくあります。今まで日本でミャンマー音楽を紹介する時はワールドミュージックの文脈で語られることがほとんどでしたが、それはあまりに惜しい。その独自性はフリージャズ/プログレ/現代音楽/ダブにも通じます。
2016年から現地調査を行っている村上巨樹が、レコード、カセット、CD、映像などを流しながらその歴史と魅力を紹介します。特に今回は、2023年12月に行った現地調査の結果(ノイズシーンやレコード掘り)の初報告があります。
知られざる超絶音楽大国・ミャンマーの衝撃を味わってください。
　
　会場：art space tetra
　2024年9月13日（金）
　時間：19:00開始（30分前開場）
